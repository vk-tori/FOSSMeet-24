About FOSSMeet

FOSSMeet is the longest student Free and Open Source Software event in India. It is a platform for FOSS enthusiasts to come together and share their knowledge and ideas. The event is organized by Computer Science and Engineering Association (CSEA) and FOSSCell NITC, the FOSS club of NIT Calicut.
